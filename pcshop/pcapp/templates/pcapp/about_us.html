Our Story

Building the future of PC Hardware since 2010

Our Journey

PC Hardware Shop was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide high-quality computer hardware and exceptional service to tech enthusiasts, gamers, and professionals.

2010

The Beginning

Started as a small shop in Noida with just two employees and a passion for technology.

2015

Expansion

Expanded to three physical locations across NCR and launched our online store to serve customers nationwide.

2020

Today

Now a leading PC hardware retailer with over 50 employees, serving thousands of satisfied customers across India.

Quality
